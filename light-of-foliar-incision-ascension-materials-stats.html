Genshin Impact Light of Foliar Incision Ascension Materials, Stats

Increase Light of Foliar Incision's stats in Genshin Impact by ascending its stats with these ascension materials.

First Published January 18, 2023, 10:08

Genshin Impact 3.4 update is just a few days away, and it's set to bring a brand new weapon called Light of Foliar Incision. It's a five-star Sword specifically developed for the new Dendro character, Alhaitham.

If you are able to grab the weapon from its banner,  you must ascend Light of Foliar Incision stats to get the best out of it. Luckily, HoYoverse has officially announced the Light of Foliar Incision ascension materials and stats so that travelers can pre-farm the materials and upgrade the Sword right away.

Light of Foliar Incision Weapon Stats

  • Rarity: 5 Star
  • Conversion Exp: 300000
  • Base Attack: 44.34
  • Substat Type: Critical Damage %
  • Base Substat: 19.2%
  • Weapon Affix: Whitemoon Bristle
  • Affix Description: CRIT Rate is increased by 4%. When Normal Attacks deal Elemental DMG, the Foliar Incision effect will be obtained, increasing DMG dealt by Normal Attacks and Elemental Skills by 120% of Elemental Mastery. This effect will disappear after 28 DMG instances or 12s. You can obtain Foliar Incision once every 12s.

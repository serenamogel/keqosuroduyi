27 Veterans Day captions for Instagram to celebrate real heroes on social media

March 29, 2024
blog

There’s nothing like a well-thought-out caption with a throwback picture on Instagram to honor Armed Force personnel on the occasion of Veterans Day in 2023.

November 11 is observed as Veterans Day every year in the United States, and the celebrations for the day are marked by a series of events, including parades, freebies at restaurants, and even discounts on online shopping. Social media will be full of praises and quotes to pay respects to the real heroes who dedicated their lives to serve the country. A beautiful picture on Instagram with an equally captivating caption is just the perfect addition to celebrations that have existed for ages.

Grab free meals, coffee and more in 2023

Several restaurants are giving away freebies to honor Veterans on Saturday, November 11. However, the deals at the below restaurants are only open to active duty members or veterans. You can participate by showing your military ID.

  • Applebee’s is offering a free full-size entrée while dining in.
  • Aroma Joe’s is giving away a free drink.
  • Arby’s at multiple locations are giving free Classic Roast Beef Sandwich.
  • Buffalo Wild Wings has a free order of 10 boneless Wings and Fries for veterans and military personnel.
  • Get a free doughnut of your choice at Dunkin’.
  • IHOP is offering free Red, White, and blueberry Pancakes or a free Pancake Combo between 7 A.M. and 7 P.M.
  • Wendy’s is giving away a free breakfast combo for veterans and active-duty military

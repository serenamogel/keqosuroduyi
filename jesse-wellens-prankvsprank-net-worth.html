Jesse Michael Wellens (PrankvsPrank) Wiki Biography

PrankvsPrank is a Youtube channel started in 2009 by Jesse Michael Wellens with his girlfriend at the time, Jennifer “Jeana” Smith, but following their breakup in 2016, Jesse is the only admin on the channel. Jesse was born on the 25th September 1982 in Riverton, New Jersey USA, and has used the popularity of the channel PrankvsPrank to increase his net worth.

Have you ever wondered how rich Jesse Wellens is, as of late 2017? According to authoritative sources, Jesse Wellens has acquired a total wealth of $5 million, primarily thanks to his successful YouTube channel, but also through other ventures, such as film production.

Before starting the channel, Jesse and Jeana dated for four years; their first video was the cinnamon challenge, and after getting attention for this particular video, it sparked a series of similar videos, which only encouraged the two to continue with their work. Jeana is now the owner of this channel, and has continued uploading videos despite their breakup.

Aside from PrankvsPrank, Jesse is also a producer of short films, based out of Venice Beach, California, which has also increased Jesse’s net worth, though he has also resided in New York City since his breakup from Jeana.

Regarding Jesse’s personal life, he has been single since breaking up with Jeana, and is apparently focused entirely on building his solo career.

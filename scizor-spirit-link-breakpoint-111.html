Scizor Spirit Link
ハッサムソウルリンク Hassam Soul Link

Scizor Spirit Link (Japanese: ハッサムソウルリンク Hassam Soul Link) is a Pokémon Tool card. It is part of the BREAKpoint expansion.

Card text

Attach a Pokémon Tool to 1 of your Pokémon that doesn't already have a Pokémon Tool attached to it.
Your turn does not end if the Pokémon this card is attached to becomes M Scizor-EX.
You may play as many Item cards as you like during your turn (before your attack).

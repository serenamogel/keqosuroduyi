Leann Craumer is a Librarian from Catonsville, Maryland, United States. She is currently serving as a Librarian at Anne Arundel County Public Library since September 2016.

Now, Leann is looking forward to testing her trivia skills with participation in the 39th season of America’s longest-running quiz game show, Jeopardy! She is set to make her debut on 17 July 2023.

Leann will make her Jeopardy! debut on 17 July 2023 (Source: Anne Arundel County Public Library’s Facebook)

Talking about her inclusion in the most popular trivia show, Leanna wrote on her Facebook on 5 July 2023,

Watch me on national television – Monday, July 17!

A couple months ago I had the amazing opportunity to fly to Culver City, CA and be a contestant on Jeopardy!, a literal dream come true for me as many of you know. It was one of the most exhilarating and terrifying things I’ve ever done, and I can’t wait to see it. I’m planning to host a Zoom watch party if you’d like to join me – link will be posted closer to air date.

You can check for your local station and broadcast time here: https://www.jeopardy.com/watch

Leann was trying to be part of Jeopardy! since 2016. She started taking online tests but only got past the next round for the first time in 2022. Eventually, Leann took a Zoom audition in July 2022.

As Leann is set to make her debut on Monday, she will face the current one-day champion, Daniel Moore, and a retired elementary teacher from Fanwood, New Jersey, Jerry Powers.

Daniel, a Contract Compliance Analyst from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania became the new winner on 14 July 2023. With the victory, he added $25,000 to his winning purse.

It will be interesting to see if Leann can outlast her opponents and grab a memorable win on her Jeopardy! debut. Don’t forget to watch the new episode on Monday, 17 July 2023.

Besides English, Leann is also fluent in the Italian language. She learned the Italian language at Italiaidea in 2009.

Leann Craumer Job Details

Leann is serving as a Librarian for quite some time now. Currently, she is a Librarian at Anne Arundel County Public Library. She took the role back in September 2016 and has been working at the library for 6 years and 11 months now.

Prior to that, Leann worked as a Librarian I – Children’s Services at Enoch Pratt Free Library & Maryland State Library Resource Center from November 2015 to September 2016. She was also a Program Coordinator at John Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health from March 2012 to November 2015.

Similarly, Leann served as a Library Assistant at the University of Maryland School of Law from July 2011 to June 2012. Before that, she worked as a Research Assistant / Clinic Coordinator at Kennedy Krieger Institute from June 2010 to July 2011.

Leann commenced her professional career working as a Page/Clerk at East Rockaway Public Library from October 2004 to April 2010. She was also a Manager at Rita’s Italian Ice from June 2006 to August 2008.

On top of that, Leann also had a teaching experience at The Answer SAT Prep from August 2010 to June 2011.

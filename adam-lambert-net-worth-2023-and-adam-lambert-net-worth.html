Adam Lambert Net Worth 2023: A Look at the Career and Earnings of the Famous Musician

Early Life and Career

Adam Lambert was born in Indianapolis, Indiana, in 1982. He grew up in San Diego, California, and attended college at California State University, Fullerton. He started performing in musicals and theatre productions while in college, and eventually moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career in music.

In 2009, Adam Lambert auditioned for the eighth season of American Idol, where he quickly became a fan favorite.

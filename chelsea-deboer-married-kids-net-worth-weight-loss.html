Who Is Chelsea Deboer? Family Details

Chelsea DeBoer was born on 29 August 1991 in Vermillion, South Dakota, USA, as Chelsea Anne Houska to parents Randy and Mary Houska. She has three siblings; Angie, Emily, and Melissa Houska.

Chelsea was a popular softball player and a high school senior in Stewart School who then dropped out of school when she became pregnant by her then-boyfriend, Adam Lind. Chelsea had a difficult pregnancy and gave birth to Aubree five weeks early in September 2009, on the first day of her senior year of high school. Chelsea later separated from Adam in 2012 to provide Aubree a stable family life.

After Aubree’s birth, Chelsea took online classes to finish her degree. She then earned her GED and got enrolled in beauty school. Then she got a part-time job in a salon and later attained a degree in cosmetology in November 2013.

The 29-year-old TV personality stands at the height of 5 ft 3 in or 160 cm and weighs 55 kg or 121 lbs. She has got a slim body appearance.

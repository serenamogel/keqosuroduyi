Charlie Robison Children: Meet Charles Augustus Robison, Julianna Tex Robison &amp; Henry Benjamin Robis

June 21, 2024
blog

Charlie Robison had three children:  Charles Augustus, and twins, Julianna Tex and Henry Benjamin. There isn’t much information about his children on the internet as they like to keep their lives away from the limelight.

In 2003, Robison was a judge on the first season of the TV singing competition, Nashville Star.

Unhappy with the expectations and limitations of being a Nashville country artist, he moved to a smaller independent label, Dualton, for Good Times in 2004, followed by extensive touring and newfound control over his career.

Five years after the release of Good Times, Robison released Beautiful Day on June 23, 2009, on Dualtone. This was the first CD he self-produced. Both albums featured several songs written by Nashville singer-songwriter, Keith Gattis.

Robison resumed his music career in 2022. He returned to Billy Bob’s as part of his first tour since 2018, playing at the same venue where he first played in 1999.

Career

An American lyricist who wrote some of the most iconic songs in the English language of the twentieth century with his younger brother, composer George Gershwin. 1st

He co-wrote almost a dozen Broadway plays with George, including songs like “I Got Rhythm,” “Embraceable You,” “The Man I Love,” and “Someone to Watch Over Me.” He also wrote the text for George’s opera Porgy and Bess, along with DuBose Heyward.
With composers Jerome Kern, Kurt Weill, Harry Warren, and Harold Arlen, he wrote more popular tunes.
